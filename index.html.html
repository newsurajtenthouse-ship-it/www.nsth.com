About Us

Crafting Dream Events
Since 2014

Welcome to New Suraj Tent House & Rental Services — Sarlahi's premier event planning, decoration, and rental company. Founded in 2014 by Dharmendra Chaudhary, we have proudly served the people of Harion and across the Sarlahi District for over 12 years.

With a dedicated team of 20+ professionals, we specialize in transforming venues into breathtaking spaces. From traditional Nepali ceremonies to modern celebrations, every event receives our full passion and attention to detail.

न्यू सूरज टेन्ट हाउस एण्ड रेन्टल सर्भिसेजमा स्वागत छ। धर्मेन्द्र चौधरीको नेतृत्वमा सन् २०१४ मा स्थापित, हामी सर्लाहीको सर्वश्रेष्ठ इभेन्ट प्लानिङ कम्पनी हौं। हाम्रा २०+ पेशेवर टोलीले हरेक कार्यक्रमलाई यादगार बनाउँछ।
